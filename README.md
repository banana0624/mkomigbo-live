# Mkomigbo

**Open knowledge and heritage platform for Igbo history, culture, language, and people.**

Live at: [mkomigbo.com](https://mkomigbo.com)

---

## What is Mkomigbo?

Mkomigbo is a free, open knowledge platform documenting the history, culture, language,
religion, and heritage of the Igbo people of West Africa — and the broader African
experience that connects them to the world.

The name draws from the Igbo language: gathering knowledge from the forest.

---

### AWAG — Africa Weekly Activities Guide

A practical seasonal calendar for African communities covering farming, fishing, trading,
herding, healing, and community life. 13 live communities across 9 ecological zones.
Works offline. Installable on any device.

Live at: [mkomigbo.com/awag/](https://mkomigbo.com/awag/)
